What Are CPD Hours?

Continuing Professional Development hours are mandatory for ICPAU-licensed accountants to maintain their practising certificates each year.

🎯

Annual Requirement

ICPAU members must complete 40 verifiable CPD hours annually. Each Base Associates short course earns you structured CPD credit.

📜

Instant Certificates

Download your CPD certificate immediately after completing any course — ready for submission to ICPAU.
